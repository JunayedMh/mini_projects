# Are these words anagram?

**Write a function that takes two strings and returns True if they are anagrams of each other, and False otherwise. An anagram is a word formed by rearranging the letters of a different word (like cinema and iceman).**

The Twist:

- Your function must ignore spaces and capitalization.

Input example: "Listen" and "Silent " (notice the trailing space and capital letter).

Expected Output: True/Result
